Shrimp Tacos

This delicious dinner feast will be savory; you will be wanting them
everyday. (Pescatarian friendly meal)

Ingredients

  • 1 mango-peeled, seeded ,and diced
  • 1 ripe avocada-peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/4 cup honey butter
  • 1 pound salad shrimp
  • 4 (10inch) flour tortillas, warmed

Steps

  1. Toss the mango, avocado, tomatoes, cilantro, onion, garlic, salt, and lime juice together in a bowl. Cover and refridgerate for 30 minutes
  2. Melt the honey butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp; cook and stiruntil pink and opaque, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. To serve, place a few shrimp onto a warm tortilla, top with mango salsa and fold up. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
